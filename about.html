What we Do

M-Soma equips high school graduates in Kenya with the fundamental coding skills and mentorship that would enable them to access careers in technology.

Summer Bootcamp
A 4 week program targeted for beginners. With 150 hours of project-based learning, and a host of group activities, the bootcamp is a true intensive program covering courses ranging from problem solving and programming, to ethical and social issues in computing.
E-Learning App
Optimized for fast loading times to deal with slow internet speeds. Content is available for download, and uses HTML5 local storage to allow learners to view materials offline.
Volunteer Opportunity
Our courses are taught by computer science scholars and professionals on a volunteer basis, ensuring that our students have exposure to role models and potential mentors in the field.
